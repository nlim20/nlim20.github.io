+ 4-Bar Window Cycler (Tesla)

- Developed and utilized linkage synthesis tools in C to generate a 4-bar geometry capable of cycling a manual side window with an irregular handle sweep

- Achieved effective performance with a single actuator and straightforward controls


+ Overhead Strength Testing Gantry (Tesla)

- Engineered a vertical gantry system capable of applying high loads to every +z surface on any passenger vehicle or pickup truck
- Structural design was driven by seismic loads and deflection criteria at the end effector


+ Cinch Cable Durability Test Rig (Tesla)

- Created a test rig and control system for the simultaneous durability cycling of 12 individual cinch cables
- Incorporated continuous data collection and failure detection for each cable independently


+ Cadillac LYRIQ Subframe and Chassis Structure (EcoCAR)

- Designed a custom chassis structure and mounting/packaging strategy for an energy-efficient electric powertrain in a Cadillac LYRIQ
- Managed top-level vehicle CAD, including reverse engineering of the physical vehicle through 3D scanning
- Conducted detailed structural analyses to ensure the added structures met competition-specified strength and stiffness requirements


+ Functional Model of EV Thermal Management System (EcoCAR)

- Designed and constructed a working model of a thermal management system for a Cadillac LYRIQ
- Utilized a heating element to mimic drive unit heat generation, with heat dissipation managed by a pump-radiator-fan system controlled via Arduino
- Project was used as a test platform for different thermal control strategies and as an educational resource for new team members.


+ Hybrid Electric Go-Kart (Personal Project)
